WHO WE ARE ?

Ensuring Your Success Through Reliable Solutions

Established in 2017, Himil uwave Researchco private limited has been concentrating on providing customers in the Defense, Aerospace, Industrial, and Automotive business segments with value-adding services in the field of ESDM. Our team of industry specialists oversees state-of-the-art lab and manufacturing facilities, enabling us to lead the way in RF, Power Electronics, Embedded & IoT solutions, and Manufacturing services. Hicotronics, its parent company, provides HIMIL with sourcing power, which it uses to establish a state-of-the-art manufacturing plant that can produce and deliver goods and services with superior durability.

  • Building trust is at the heart of HIMIL's success. Through strong relationships with customers and partners, they've established a track record of exceeding expectations and achieving consistent growth.
  • HIMIL boasts a proven track record of exceptional execution ability.

Vision

Create Differentiation as a partner in ESDM (Electronics System Design & Manufacturing) enabling Customers productize concepts.

Mission

Drive Innovation in design and manufacturing with focus on Quality, Service ability, Sustainability & Employee well-being.

Team story

We at HIMIL are passionate about innovation and work hard to lead the way in the ESDM industry. Experts in the fields of RF engineering, power electronics, embedded systems, and Internet of Things solutions make up our team. Using our cutting-edge lab and manufacturing facilities, we collaborate closely with clients to provide high-quality manufacturing services. Our staff is dedicated to quality and client happiness, therefore we make sure that our solutions satisfy your particular company requirements. Join us in advancing industry-wide technological transformation.

Awards & Certifications

Quality is the first priority for HIMIL. Our values and approach are based on the firm conviction that customer satisfaction and trust are unassailable. Our EMS operations have achieved ISO 9001, AS9100D, and IATF quality system certifications with this methodology. With an emphasis on environmentally friendly operations, ISO 14001 certification is being worked on and is anticipated to be finished by July 24.
